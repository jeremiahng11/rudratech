The Founder

About Jeremiah.

Jeremiah Ng is a serial co-founder and full-stack CTO/CIO based in Singapore, and the engineer behind Rudra Tech Pte Ltd. He builds products end-to-end — architecture, backend, mobile, web and the applied-AI layer on top.

His work spans Generative AI, LLMs and RAG systems, full-stack development across Python/Django, React, React Native, Flutter and Node.js, cloud infrastructure on AWS, GCP and Azure, and blockchain. Today he runs Rudra Tech, founded SkenPay Ltd (Hong Kong), and serves as Group CTO & CIO at Aleta Planet.

Earlier chapters include SoReal Prop (acquired), Hook Tech, incubation work at Google (Viztree), and early days at Streetsine / SRX — giving him a rare blend of startup building, enterprise delivery and hands-on engineering.
